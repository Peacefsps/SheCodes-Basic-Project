I love cakes!

Especially cupcakes, a bite of delight!

A cupcake also known as a fairy cake or bun is a small cake designed to serve one person, which may be baked in a small thin paper or aluminum cup. As with larger cakes, frosting and other cake decorations such as fruit and candy may be applied.

My absolute favourite flavour of cupcake is Chocolatey. What is more satisfying than a rich chocolatey dessert!
